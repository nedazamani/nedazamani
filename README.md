# Hi there, I'm Neda Zamani 👋

### Product Manager | Background in Product Design Leadership & Data-Driven Solutions

I am a product-minded leader with over 8 years of experience driving measurable growth across FinTech and AdTech products. Transitioning from a strong foundation in Product Design Leadership to Product Management, I specialize in bridging the gap between business strategy, user experience, and engineering to deliver impactful, scalable solutions.

My core philosophy centers on discovering true user needs, aligning them with business goals, and defining clear, metrics-driven product roadmaps. 

---

### 🚀 What I Do
- **Product Strategy & Vision:** Defining roadmaps, prioritizing features using diverse frameworks, and aligning cross-functional teams with business objectives.
- **Data-Driven Discovery:** Leveraging user research, market analysis, and product analytics to make informed, objective decisions.
- **AI-Driven Workflows:** Integrating AI capabilities into product pipelines to optimize processes and enhance the core user experience.
- **Design Leadership:** Utilizing my background in UX and industrial design to ensure products are not only functional but inherently human-centered.

### 🛠️ Core Competencies

- **Focus Areas:** SaaS, FinTech, AdTech, B2B/B2C Solutions
